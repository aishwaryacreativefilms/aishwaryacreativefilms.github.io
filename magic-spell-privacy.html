Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 11, 2025
App Name: Magic Spelling
Developer/Company Name: Aishwarya Creative Films LLC
Website: https://www.aishwaryacreativefilms.com/
Contact Email: admin@aishwaryacreativefilms.com

At Magic Spelling, developed by Aishwarya Creative Films LLC, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy describes how your data is (or is not) collected, used, and shared when you use our app.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Physical address
  • Contact list
  • Login credentials

Our app does not require user accounts or authentication. We do not store or transmit any personal data through our servers.

2. Third-Party Services

While we do not collect any personal information ourselves, Magic Spelling integrates the following trusted third-party services that may collect non-personal data for specific purposes:

a. Unity LevelPlay (Advertising)

We use Unity LevelPlay to display ads within the app. LevelPlay may collect:

  • Device identifiers
  • Advertising IDs
  • Location (coarse)
  • App usage data
  • IP addresses (anonymized)

This information is used to serve relevant ads and monitor ad performance. For more details on Unity's practices, please visit: https://www.is.com/privacy-policy/

You may opt out of personalized ads through your device settings.

b. Statsig (Analytics and Feature Enablement)

We use Statsig to understand how users interact with the app and to enable or test new features. Statsig may collect:

  • Device type and OS version
  • App usage patterns
  • Crash and error reports
  • Country and language settings

This data is anonymous and used only to improve app performance and feature quality. Statsig’s privacy policy is available at: https://statsig.com/privacy

3. Children’s Privacy

Magic Spelling is designed for general audiences, including children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child has submitted personal data, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to remove it.

4. Data Retention

Since we do not collect personal data, we do not retain any personally identifiable information. Third-party services may retain anonymized usage data according to their own retention policies.

5. Data Security

We take data security seriously. Although we do not collect or store any sensitive information, we partner only with reputable third-party services that follow industry-standard practices for data security and encryption.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may occasionally update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in technology or applicable laws. We will notify users of any significant changes via an update in the app or a revised policy posted on our website with a new “Effective Date.”

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or feedback about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us:
