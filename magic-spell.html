Why You'll Love It

Whether you're a beginner or already a word wizard, Magic Spelling offers learning through play with parent-approved features. No login required!

  • Create custom word lists with your own voice or a friendly robot voice.
  • Practice and test modes that make spelling fun and interactive.
  • Print words to paper or PDF in multiple formats.
  • Interactive spelling games with delightful mascots and themes.
  • Progress tracking and scorecards to celebrate improvement.
  • Optional AI-generated images to spark imagination.

Ads can be turned off at any time, and the app works offline once your word lists are ready.

Read the Magic Spelling privacy policy.

How It Works

  1. Create or import a word list and choose a fun voice to read the words aloud.
  2. Practice each word at your own pace, then switch to Test mode when you're ready for a challenge.
  3. Earn stars and unlock themed mascots as you master new spelling lists.
  4. Track progress with printable scorecards to share with parents or teachers.
