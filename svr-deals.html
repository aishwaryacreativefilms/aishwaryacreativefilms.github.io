SVR Deals – Black Friday App

SVR Deals is built for people who love great prices but hate wasting time digging through cluttered threads, expired coupons, and paywalled flyers.

Most deals are hand-picked, reviewed, and refined so you see what’s actually worth your attention—not every random price drop.

No Signups. No BS. Just Deals.

Our mission is to strip away the noise and spotlight curated, trustworthy drops. Launch the app and immediately dive into the best prices across tech, food, fashion, home, and more without creating an account.

Save favorites locally, opt in to notifications only if you want a gentle nudge, and browse an honest, lightweight experience tuned for fast scanning.

Why SVR Deals is different

Curated, high-signal deals

Editors and the community sift through retailer drops, promos, and limited-time offers so you get the highlights, not the firehose.

Black Friday & seasonal hubs

Dedicated hubs split online, in-store, and ad-scan flyers. Preview PDF scans inside the app and hop straight into matching deals.

Ongoing & daily themes

Browse “Ongoing” campaigns with smart filters like Tuesday specials, food & dining, and live campaign tags pulled from config.

Smart, helpful search

Search titles, tags, categories, and retailers at once, then refine with “browse by tag” and “browse by category” controls.

Starred tags

Favorite up to ten tags—think “Top 20”, “Black Friday”, or “restaurants”—to build a personalized Starred Tags screen.

Deal pages with context

Each deal expands into pricing, discount, timing, in-store vs online info, ad scans, and store-specific savings tips.

Easy-access coupons

Required codes are highlighted and copy to your clipboard with one tap so checkout stays painless.

Thoughtful theming

Switch between Blue Ocean, Glass Aurora, Forest Canopy, or Citrus Pop themes that keep everything readable and accessible.

Fast, modern experience

A modern stack with caching, pagination, and pull-to-refresh makes it easy to jump in, scan what’s new, and get back to your day.

Experience highlights at a glance

  • Deal detail pages that tell the full story: Pricing context, timing, store insights, and tagged companions make each card instantly useful.
  • Easy onboarding: No account required to browse or save favorites locally, with optional notifications and transparent privacy controls.
  • Live transparency: Third-party integrations like analytics or ads honor App Tracking Transparency, and we only collect IP, diagnostics, and aggregated engagement data.

What happens when you open the app

  1. Start in the Black Friday hub to browse online, in-store, or flyer-specific feeds curated by editors.
  2. Use smart search and “browse by tag” to jump to the categories or retailers that matter most.
  3. Star your favorite themes to unlock a dedicated Starred Tags view for lightning-fast scanning.
  4. Tap into deal detail pages for ad scans, coupon codes, store-specific notes, and instant copy-to-clipboard helpers.
